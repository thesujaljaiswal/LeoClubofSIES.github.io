Gharkul Rally
On the 9th of December 2022, the Leo Club of SIES had its first fellowship event in collaboration with Gharkul, a school for special children. The event began with a rally organized by the school, which started from Gharkul in Santacruz and ended at Chimanlal School and Raje Sambhaji School. Volunteers from the Leo Club of SIES were present to help with the smooth functioning of the rally. Helping physically or mentally challenged people is no less than a blessing. Lending a helping hand is a core value of the Leo Club of SIES, and it was heartening to see so many members of the club come forward to support this event. The rally was a great opportunity to spread awareness about different types of disabilities and the ways in which different professions can help disabled children. The rally was a success, and the students from Gharkul were happy to participate in such an event. The Leo Club of SIES volunteers helped in guiding the students and ensuring that everything went smoothly. The students were excited to see so many people come out to support them, and they cheered and clapped as they walked along the route. The Leo Club of SIES believes that it is essential to support and encourage special children and their families. By organizing such events, they aim to raise awareness and promote inclusivity in society. The Leo Club of SIES firmly believes that every child deserves equal opportunities, regardless of their abilities, and that it is our responsibility to ensure that they receive the necessary support and care. The rally was not just about raising awareness, but it was also a great opportunity for the students from Gharkul to interact with other students and members of the community. The Leo Club of SIES volunteers were able to engage with the students, and it was a great opportunity to learn more about their challenges and how they overcome them. Overall, the fellowship event organized by the Leo Club of SIES in collaboration with Gharkul was a success. It was heartening to see so many people come together to support the special children and their families. The event was a great opportunity to spread awareness about the different types of disabilities and the ways in which we can support and help special children. The Leo Club of SIES believes that such events are essential to promote inclusivity and equality in society. They plan to organize more events in the future, and they hope to inspire more people to come forward and support special children and their families. They believe that every child deserves equal opportunities and that it is our responsibility to ensure that they receive the necessary support and care. In conclusion, the fellowship event organized by the Leo Club of SIES in collaboration with Gharkul was a beautiful example of the power of community and inclusivity. The rally was a great opportunity to raise awareness about disabilities and the ways in which we can support and help special children. The Leo Club of SIES believes that such events are essential to promote equality and inclusivity in society and plans to organize more events in the future. By working together, we can create a world that is more inclusive and supportive of everyone, regardless of their abilities.
