Christmas Elves

Summary

On the 21st of December, the Leo Club of SIES organized a community service event at the Mahatma Phule School in Dharavi. The aim of the event was to bring some holiday cheer to the students and to give the volunteers and participants a meaningful and rewarding experience.

The event started with Christmas carols being sung by the students and volunteers. The carols were sung in an interactive manner, and the students joined in, creating a festive and joyful atmosphere. The volunteers also organized group games, which were enjoyed by the students.

At the end of the event, the kids were given Christmas treats as a token of love. The joy and excitement on their faces was priceless, and it was evident that the event had brought a lot of happiness and cheer to their lives.

In addition to organizing the Christmas caroling event, the Leo Club of SIES also conducted a donation drive in their college for a week. They collected around 150+ clothes for the ones in need. The clothes were cleaned, sorted, and checked, and later on, they were donated to the school. They were handed to the faculty and will be given to the students in need by the in-charge.

The Leo Club of SIES feels humbled by the platform and the opportunity provided to them by the school committee and the school. They believe that it is essential to give back to society and to help those in need. By organizing such events and drives, they aim to create a more inclusive and supportive society, where everyone is given equal opportunities and support.

Overall, the community service event organized by the Leo Club of SIES at the Mahatma Phule School was a great success. It was heartening to see so many people come together to support the students and to spread some holiday cheer. The event was a great opportunity to give back to society and to make a positive impact in the lives of others.

The Leo Club of SIES believes that such events are essential to promote inclusivity and equality in society. They plan to organize more events in the future, and they hope to inspire more people to come forward and support those in need. By working together, we can create a world that is more inclusive and supportive of everyone, regardless of their background or circumstances.

In conclusion, the community service event organized by the Leo Club of SIES at the Mahatma Phule School was a beautiful example of the power of community and inclusivity. The event brought joy and cheer to the lives of the students, and it was a meaningful and rewarding experience for the volunteers and participants. The Leo Club of SIES believes that such events are essential to create a more inclusive and supportive society, and they plan to continue organizing such events in the future.
