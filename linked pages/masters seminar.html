Masters Seminar report

Summary

Leo Club of Sies in collab with Qs Abroad conducted a seminar for students wanting to pursue degree abroad on 4th December 2022 at The President hotel This seminar was a great starting point for students who are considering pursuing a Master's degree abroad, but are unsure about the steps involved and the challenges they may face.

The seminar started with an introduction to the various universities and countries that offer Masters degrees in the students' field of interest. The seminar highlighted the benefits of studying abroad, including exposure to different cultures, the opportunity to learn a new language, and the potential for greater career prospects upon graduation. The seminar also covered the differences between Masters programs in different countries, including the duration, requirements, and teaching styles.

The application process for Masters degree abroad was overwhelming for students, particularly if they are not familiar with the process. The seminar provided a step-by-step guide to the application process, including the required documentation, language proficiency tests, and scholarship opportunities. Additionally, the seminar provided tips on how to write a successful personal statement, and how to approach letters of recommendation.

The seminar also covered important topics such as the cost of studying abroad, and how to finance a Masters degree. The seminar discussed various funding options, including scholarships, grants, and loans, and provide guidance on how to create a budget for living expenses while studying abroad. The seminar also highlighted the potential for part-time work during the Masters program, and how to navigate the job market in the host country.

Finally, the seminar provided a platform for current Masters students who have studied abroad to share their experiences and offer advice to prospective students. These students would share their challenges, triumphs, and insights on how to make the most of the study abroad experience. This was a great educational seminar for our members.
