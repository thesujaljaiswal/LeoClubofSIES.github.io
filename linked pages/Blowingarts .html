Blowing arts event
Date: - 17/11/2022 Event: - Bonding Session 1.0 Time: - 11:00 to 1pm Venue: - Gharkul school for special kids , Santacruz Audience: - Total 25 Leo Club of Sies on November 17, 2022, had their First Arts event ( blowing art) at Gharkul school for special kids , Santacruz Before the start of the event our volunteers received a quick instructions and allocation of duties from the arts Avenue Head Leo Ritesh Once the setup was completed by 3:33pm, we asked the children to settle down and Leo Ritesh began instructing them on what to do as our other Art Director Leo Palak and the other volunteers were setting up the supplies. Ritesh then demonstrated the procedure on how just using a small paint drop ,and with the help of staw we can make a complete painting. Each volunteer had two students to instruct, help in, and complete the enjoyable art project, and some volunteers assisted with distributing and making sure no mess occurs. After finishing the artwork on which we had written the names, we set it aside to dry. After that we played games like "Break the Pyramid" and made the dance to the music on their hearts content . We also gave out chocolates to everyone as a return gift and special chocolates to the winners. After that, the children showed their gratitude by giving us thank-you cards that they made themselves. At the end of the day we sure did feel this " Children with special needs come into our lives ,leaving footprints on our hearts ,and we are never the same. "
