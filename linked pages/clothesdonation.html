23 aashayein project (clothes donation )
On 23rd November 2022 at 10:00 am the Leo Club of sies organized a Donation Drive for needy Children. The Number of Leos present for the event were 15. The event was organized Under the Global Services Team Leader Leo Vedant Vartak and Global Action Team Leader Leo Diya Furia. Under the Project Aashayein the main Purpose of the event was to Provide the Children with Exciting Gifts and donations and have a quality time with them. The Event started with an interaction session with Kids. The event begins with a small Music concert for the kids. Then followed by several interactive Games like “guess the action”, “passing The Parcel” and children Performing their talent on stage. Every participant was Provided with exciting gifts during the event. After all these activities and games, the donation drive started. Approximately 110 students were benefited by the donation drive. The kids were provided with Pouches, bottles, Stationery, notebooks and other necessary items. This donation drive was concluded at 12.30 pm with a bright smile on everyone’s face Clothes donation is extremely important for several reasons. First and foremost, it provides clothing to children who may not have access to new or gently used clothes due to financial limitations or other reasons. By donating clothes, individuals can help ensure that children have warm, clean clothing to wear, which can have a positive impact on their overall health and well-being. Secondly, clothes donation can also help to reduce waste and promote sustainability. Many people discard clothes that are still in good condition simply because they no longer fit or are no longer in style. By donating these clothes instead of throwing them away, individuals can help reduce the amount of clothing that ends up in landfills, which can have a negative impact on the environment. And as Anne Frankhas truly said "No one has ever become poor by giving." let us all come together and keep donating for a greater cause.
