Open Mic Event
On 16 December 2022, the Leo Club of SIES College of Science, Arts and Commerce hosted an open mic event that was open to all students, regardless of whether they were part of the Leo Club or not. The event was a roaring success, with a packed audience and participants who were eager to showcase their talents. The energy of the students was palpable and infectious, with an atmosphere that was charged with excitement. The event saw a diverse range of performances covering numerous genres, from Hindi classical to rock and soulful music. The performances were back-to-back, and the audience was treated to an array of performances that showcased the participants' skill set and talents. One of the highlights of the event was the guitar performances, which were mesmerizing and soulful. The participants who played the guitar showed incredible skill, playing a range of styles and genres that left the audience spellbound. It was evident that they had spent countless hours honing their craft and perfecting their technique. Another standout performance was the rap performances, which were delivered with an intensity and passion that was hard to ignore. The participants had clearly put a lot of effort into their lyrics and delivery, and it showed in their performances. The audience was blown away by the skill and talent of the performers, and there was a palpable sense of excitement and anticipation as each rapper took to the stage. The shayaris were also a highlight of the event, with participants delivering heartfelt and emotional poetry that touched the hearts of everyone in the audience. The shayaris were beautifully written, and the performers delivered them with an intensity and emotion that left a lasting impression on the audience. Overall, the open mic event was a resounding success, and it showcased the incredible talent that exists among the students of SIES College of Science, Arts and Commerce. The Leo Club of SIES College of Science, Arts and Commerce deserves commendation for organizing such an event, which not only provided a platform for students to showcase their talents but also fostered a sense of community among the students. The event was a testament to the power of music and art to bring people together and create a sense of belonging. The audience was diverse, with students from different backgrounds and cultures coming together to celebrate the talents of their peers. It was a beautiful example of the power of art to unite people and transcend cultural barriers. In conclusion, the open mic event hosted by the Leo Club of SIES College of Science, Arts and Commerce was a resounding success. The participants showcased incredible talent and skill, and the audience was left spellbound by the performances. The event was a beautiful example of the power of music and art to bring people together, and it is a testament to the talent and creativity of the students of SIES College of Science, Arts and Commerce.
