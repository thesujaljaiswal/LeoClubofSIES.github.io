29 Nov - beatboxing event
The Leo Club of sies hosted their first beat boxing workshop ( beatronics) President Leo Abel Aricat began the event with the Leo pledge and a welcome speech Then we had our music director leo Alanjo Ajay briefing us with the main purpose of the event and another music director Leo Akash Shetty introduced Mr. Sahil Gupta and offered some interesting information about his Experiences and Achievements Sahil Gupta presented his first smooth performance and then he started with defining beatboxing, its history, and taught the audience some basic killer beats. Our directors also infused the crowd with vitality, giving them the self-assurance to even perform on stage, which astounded everyone. and they thoroughly enjoyed themselves while learning new abilities All of this was followed by the outstanding performance of leo Akash Shetty and Sahil Gupta. Undoubtedly, it was a fantastic performance, and leo Alanjo Ajay gave a solo performance which was truly one of his best performances while also sharing a beat that he had learned while performing. The basic aim of beatboxing is all about having fun and being creative With the delivery of certificates to each participant and the taking of a group portrait, the event was brought to an end.
